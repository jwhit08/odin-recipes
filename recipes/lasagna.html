Lasagna Recipe

Description

This timeless lasagna recipe features layers of tender noodles, rich and savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey melted mozzarella. Perfectly seasoned with Italian herbs, it’s a hearty and satisfying dish that’s ideal for family dinners or special occasions.

Simple to prepare yet packed with flavor, this comfort food classic is sure to become a favorite at your table.

Ingredients

For Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage (or a mix of both)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste

For Ricotta Mixture

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley or basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Baking Steps

  1. Cover the dish with aluminum foil (spray the underside with non-stick spray to prevent sticking).
  2. Bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes, or until the top is bubbly and golden brown.
  3. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing to serve.
