Pizza Recipe

Description

Experience the joy of crafting your very own pizza with this simple, foolproof recipe. This classic pizza begins with a soft and chewy homemade dough, perfectly golden and crisp on the edges.

Topped with rich, tangy tomato sauce, a generous layer of gooey mozzarella, and your choice of fresh toppings, it’s a customizable masterpiece that’s sure to satisfy every craving.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast (1 packet)
  • ¾ cup warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup pizza sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, etc.)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano or Italian seasoning (optional)

Baking Steps

  1. Prepare the dough: Mix ingredients, knead, and let rise for 1 hour.
  2. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  3. Roll out the dough into your desired shape.
  4. Assemble the pizza with sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  6. Slice and serve your homemade pizza.
