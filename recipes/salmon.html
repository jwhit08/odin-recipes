Salmon Recipe

Description

Elevate your dinner table with this effortlessly delicious baked salmon recipe. Tender, flaky fillets are brushed with a blend of garlic, fresh lemon, and olive oil, creating a vibrant, citrusy flavor with every bite.

A hint of dill adds a subtle herbaceous touch, while the oven does all the work to achieve perfectly cooked, juicy salmon.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or melted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried or fresh dill (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Lemon slices, for garnish

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
  2. Pat the salmon fillets dry with a paper towel and place them on the prepared baking sheet, skin-side down.
  3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil (or melted butter), minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, dill (if using), salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush the seasoning mixture evenly over the salmon fillets. Garnish with a slice of lemon on each fillet if desired.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a couple of minutes before serving.
